Social media algorithms play a crucial role in shaping the opinions of individuals. The algorithms in question, are Machine Learning algorithms which learn from respective user's content consumption patterns or from agencies' content production and preferences. 
Social algorithms are dangerous in shaping the political narratives. One, because propaganda by media outlets against diminishing a particular narrative can cause imbalanced ideological presence on social media. Moreover, hiding one side of the story completely or hushing the voice of other opinions create a loophole in the narrative. The lack of diversified opinion display on the media causes a reduction in the tolerance threshold and people become abusive towards any new opinion or belief they see other than the mainstream or their own particular ideology.
This ideological powerplay causes an imbalanced voice on the media. Hnece, social media algorithms play a powerful yet dangerous role in shaping the ideological voices.
